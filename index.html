About the Program
BSIT at STI Calamba

A deep dive into what makes our Information Technology program one of the best in the region.

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) at STI College Calamba is a four-year degree program that produces graduates with the technical competence and professional values needed in the fast-evolving world of technology.

Anchored on the ONE STI Learning Model, our BSIT program integrates classroom instruction with hands-on laboratory work, industry immersion, and values formation to ensure holistic student development.

Our graduates are equipped to pursue careers as software developers, network administrators, cybersecurity analysts, systems analysts, and IT project managers, among many other roles in the technology sector.

Program Mission

To develop globally competitive IT professionals grounded in ethical practice, technical excellence, and a commitment to lifelong learning and service to the community.

Program Vision

To be the leading BSIT program in Calabarzon, producing IT professionals who are future-ready, innovative, and socially responsible.

Program Outcomes
What You'll Learn & Achieve

By the end of the program, BSIT graduates of STI Calamba will be able to:

  • Apply knowledge of computing, mathematics, and science fundamentals to IT solutions.
  • Design, implement, and evaluate IT systems and applications to meet desired needs.
  • Analyze complex computing problems and identify appropriate solutions using computing principles.
  • Communicate effectively with a range of audiences about technical and non-technical matters.
  • Understand professional, ethical, legal, and social issues in computing practice.
  • Make informed judgments that consider societal and global impacts of computing solutions.
  • Function effectively as a member or leader of a team in IT project development.
  • Recognize the need for and an ability to engage in continuing professional development.
Curriculum Overview
4-Year Program Structure

A balanced blend of core IT subjects, specialization courses, general education, and on-the-job training.

First Year

Introduction to BSIT, fundamentals of computing, basics of programming, and the start of Java programming to build a strong IT foundation.

Second Year

Website and application design, deeper Java and Python programming, database concepts, SAP fundamentals, and the basics of C# development.

Third Year

SAP 2, database management, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for website creation, capstone system development, mobile development, and cybersecurity.

Fourth Year

Capstone Project completion, On-the-Job Training for 600 hours, IT Project Management, and professional electives that prepare students for industry work.

Frequently Asked Questions
Got Questions?

Here are the most common questions about the BSIT program at STI College Calamba.

What is the duration of the BSIT program at STI Calamba?
The BSIT program is a four-year undergraduate degree program. It includes eight semesters of coursework, a 600-hour on-the-job training (OJT), and a capstone project in the final year.
What are the admission requirements for BSIT?
Applicants must be a high school graduate (Grade 12 / SHS) or its equivalent. Requirements include a completed application form, report card (Form 138), PSA Birth Certificate, 2×2 ID photos, and passing the STI Admission Test. Some scholarship applications may require additional documents.
Are there scholarships available for BSIT students?
Yes! STI College Calamba offers several scholarship programs including the STI Academic Scholarship, government-funded scholarships (CHED, TESDA), and corporate-sponsored scholarships through our industry partners. Contact the admissions office for the latest available grants.
What career paths can BSIT graduates pursue?
BSIT graduates are equipped for careers such as Software Developer, Web Developer, Network Administrator, Cybersecurity Analyst, Database Administrator, Systems Analyst, IT Project Manager, and more. Many graduates also pursue further studies or entrepreneurship in the tech industry.
Does the program prepare students for professional IT certifications?
Yes. The BSIT curriculum at STI Calamba is aligned with globally recognized certifications including Cisco CCNA, CompTIA A+/Network+/Security+, Microsoft Certified: Azure Fundamentals, and others. Students are encouraged and assisted in taking these exams.
What is the OJT program like for BSIT students?
BSIT students complete a minimum of 600 hours of on-the-job training during their final year. STI Calamba has strong partnerships with local and national technology companies, BPOs, and government agencies that regularly accept our students. The BSIT department assists in placement and monitors OJT progress.
Is online or blended learning available?
STI College Calamba supports flexible and technology-enhanced learning. For official campus announcements, admissions information, program updates, and STI-wide services, students may visit the STI official website at sti.edu.
How can I contact the BSIT department directly?
You can reach the BSIT department through our official email at calamba@sti.edu, by calling our trunkline at (049) 545-2945 or (049) 545-7520, or by visiting the campus during office hours (Monday–Friday, 7:00 AM–6:00 PM; Saturday, 8:00 AM–5:00 PM). You may also use the contact form on our Contact Us page.
